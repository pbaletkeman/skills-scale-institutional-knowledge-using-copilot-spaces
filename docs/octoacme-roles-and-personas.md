# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

### Key Interactions
- **With Product Managers:** Clarify requirements, discuss technical constraints, and propose implementation approaches
- **With Project Managers:** Provide estimates, report blockers, and coordinate on timelines
- **With QA Lead:** Collaborate on test strategies and fix defects
- **With DevOps Engineers:** Work on deployment pipelines and infrastructure needs
- **With UX/UI Designers:** Implement designs and provide feedback on feasibility
- **With Technical Writers:** Review documentation for accuracy

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

### Key Interactions
- **With Developers:** Clarify requirements, review implementations, and validate solutions
- **With Project Managers:** Align on priorities, timelines, and resource allocation
- **With UX/UI Designers:** Define user needs and validate design solutions
- **With QA Lead:** Define acceptance criteria and success metrics
- **With Support/Customer Success:** Gather customer feedback and prioritize improvements
- **With Stakeholders:** Present roadmaps and gather business requirements

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

### Key Interactions
- **With Product Managers:** Align on priorities, scope, and trade-offs
- **With Developers:** Coordinate on timelines, remove blockers, and track progress
- **With QA Lead:** Ensure quality gates are met before releases
- **With DevOps Engineers:** Coordinate deployment schedules and infrastructure readiness
- **With Stakeholders:** Provide status updates and manage expectations
- **With All Team Members:** Facilitate meetings, track action items, and maintain documentation

---

## UX/UI Designer

### Role Summary
UX/UI Designers research user needs, design intuitive interfaces, and ensure a consistent user experience across products. They create wireframes, prototypes, and design systems that guide implementation.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Create wireframes, mockups, and interactive prototypes
- Maintain design systems and component libraries
- Collaborate with developers on implementation feasibility
- Validate designs with users and stakeholders
- Ensure accessibility and responsive design standards

### Goals
- Create intuitive, accessible user experiences
- Maintain design consistency across products
- Reduce user friction and improve satisfaction
- Balance user needs with technical constraints

### Typical Communication
- Design review sessions with stakeholders
- Collaboration sessions with developers during implementation
- User research findings and insights reports
- Design specification documentation

### Key Interactions
- **With Product Managers:** Understand user needs and business requirements
- **With Developers:** Provide design specs, review implementations, and adjust for technical constraints
- **With QA Lead:** Define expected user flows and visual requirements for testing
- **With Technical Writers:** Collaborate on user-facing documentation and help content
- **With Support/Customer Success:** Gather feedback on user pain points and usability issues

---

## Quality Assurance Lead

### Role Summary
Quality Assurance Leads define and implement testing strategies to ensure product quality, reliability, and security. They coordinate testing efforts, manage test environments, and validate that features meet acceptance criteria.

### Responsibilities
- Define test strategies and quality standards
- Create and maintain test plans and test cases
- Coordinate manual and automated testing efforts
- Manage test environments and data
- Track and triage defects
- Validate features against acceptance criteria
- Report on quality metrics and release readiness

### Goals
- Ensure high product quality and reliability
- Catch defects early in the development cycle
- Maintain comprehensive test coverage
- Enable confident, low-risk releases

### Typical Communication
- Daily standup participation and blocker reporting
- Test status reports and quality metrics dashboards
- Defect triage sessions with developers
- Release readiness assessments

### Key Interactions
- **With Developers:** Collaborate on test strategies, report defects, and verify fixes
- **With Product Managers:** Clarify acceptance criteria and validate feature behavior
- **With Project Managers:** Report on testing progress and quality gates
- **With DevOps Engineers:** Coordinate test environment setup and CI/CD integration
- **With UX/UI Designers:** Validate UI implementation against design specifications

---

## Technical Writer / Documentation Specialist

### Role Summary
Technical Writers create and maintain documentation that helps users, developers, and stakeholders understand and use the product effectively. They produce user guides, API documentation, internal process docs, and release notes.

### Responsibilities
- Write and maintain user-facing documentation
- Create API documentation and developer guides
- Produce release notes and migration guides
- Maintain internal process documentation
- Collaborate with SMEs to ensure accuracy
- Establish documentation standards and templates
- Organize and structure documentation for discoverability

### Goals
- Provide clear, accurate, and accessible documentation
- Reduce support burden through self-service content
- Ensure documentation stays current with product changes
- Improve developer and user experience through quality docs

### Typical Communication
- Documentation review sessions with SMEs
- Collaboration with developers during feature development
- Feedback collection from users and support teams
- Documentation planning discussions with Product Managers

### Key Interactions
- **With Developers:** Review technical accuracy, understand implementation details
- **With Product Managers:** Understand feature scope and target audience
- **With UX/UI Designers:** Collaborate on in-product help and user guides
- **With Support/Customer Success:** Identify documentation gaps and common questions
- **With QA Lead:** Document test procedures and quality standards

---

## DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers design, implement, and maintain the infrastructure, deployment pipelines, and automation that enable reliable and efficient software delivery. They bridge development and operations concerns.

### Responsibilities
- Design and maintain CI/CD pipelines
- Manage infrastructure as code
- Monitor system health and performance
- Implement security and compliance measures
- Automate deployment and operational tasks
- Manage environments (dev, staging, production)
- Respond to infrastructure incidents and outages
- Optimize build and deployment processes

### Goals
- Enable fast, reliable, and safe deployments
- Maintain high system availability and performance
- Automate repetitive operational tasks
- Ensure security and compliance standards

### Typical Communication
- Infrastructure planning sessions
- Incident response and post-mortems
- Deployment coordination and runbooks
- Performance and monitoring reports

### Key Interactions
- **With Developers:** Provide deployment tools, debug production issues, optimize build times
- **With Project Managers:** Coordinate deployment schedules and infrastructure changes
- **With QA Lead:** Maintain test environments and integrate automated testing
- **With Product Managers:** Align infrastructure capabilities with product roadmap
- **With Support/Customer Success:** Investigate production issues and provide incident updates

---

## Support / Customer Success

### Role Summary
Support and Customer Success teams serve as the voice of the customer, helping users successfully adopt and use the product while gathering feedback to drive improvements. They handle inquiries, troubleshoot issues, and advocate for customer needs.

### Responsibilities
- Respond to customer inquiries and support tickets
- Troubleshoot and resolve product issues
- Document and escalate bugs and feature requests
- Provide product training and onboarding
- Monitor customer health and satisfaction metrics
- Gather customer feedback and usage insights
- Advocate for customer needs in planning discussions

### Goals
- Maximize customer satisfaction and retention
- Resolve issues quickly and effectively
- Reduce churn through proactive engagement
- Provide actionable feedback to improve the product

### Typical Communication
- Daily ticket triage and escalation
- Customer feedback reports and trend analysis
- Feature request summaries for Product Managers
- Training and onboarding sessions with customers

### Key Interactions
- **With Product Managers:** Provide customer insights, prioritize pain points and feature requests
- **With Developers:** Escalate bugs, provide reproduction steps, and validate fixes
- **With QA Lead:** Report defects found in production and help with reproduction
- **With Technical Writers:** Identify documentation gaps and provide user perspective
- **With DevOps Engineers:** Coordinate on production incidents and service health
- **With UX/UI Designers:** Share user feedback on usability and pain points
